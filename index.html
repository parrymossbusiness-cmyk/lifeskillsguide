What is Gelotology? Gelotology is a branch of science studying laughter. A wide-open-mouth hearty laff expels air at up to 60 miles per hour and provides significantly more physiological and psychological benefits than a simple smile or grin, including stress hormone reduction, immune system activation, and improved mood.

What is pretend practice or mental imagery in sports? Mental imagery, also called pretend practice, involves vividly imagining the performance of a physical or behavioral skill on the "Mind's TV Screen." Decades of sports psychology research confirm that mental imagery measurably accelerates real-world skill acquisition.

The Science of Gelotology

Most people settle for a polite smile or a mild grin. But Gelotology — the formal scientific study of laughter — reveals that a wide-open-mouth hearty laff is in a completely different physiological category. Air moves through your system at speeds up to 60 miles per hour, triggering a cascade of biological responses: cortisol plummets, endorphins surge, the immune system activates.

"A genuine hearty laff isn't just fun — it's the most efficient, free, and always-available stress-management tool the human body possesses."

The Moss-Cess Success Profile

Bob Moss distilled 50 years of coaching into four non-negotiable pillars for anyone serious about success: Self-Confidence (believing in your capacity to grow), Goal Achievement (turning ambitions into executed plans), an Open Mind (staying receptive to new methods and ideas), and the transformative skill of turning Negatives into Positives. Together, these form the Moss-Cess Success Profile — your personal blueprint for peak performance.

"Success is not an accident. It has a profile. Learn the profile. Live the profile."
03

Creative Visualization & Pretend Practice

Your brain cannot reliably distinguish between a vividly imagined experience and a real one — and sports psychology has documented this for decades. Bob Moss calls it your "Mind's TV Screen." By deliberately running detailed mental rehearsals of physical skills, social interactions, and goal achievement scenarios, you accelerate real-world performance without spending a single extra hour in physical practice.

"Before you do it, see it. Before you say it, hear it. Your Mind's TV Screen is playing 24/7 — you choose the programming."
04

The Enthusiasm-Laffter Connection

The book's namesake concept: the direct, bidirectional relationship between sustained enthusiasm and regular hearty laffter. Enthusiasm generates laffter; laffter reinforces enthusiasm. This self-sustaining loop — once deliberately activated — rewires habitual thought patterns, improves interpersonal relationships, enhances physical health, and creates measurable resilience against life's inevitable setbacks.

"Enthusiasm and laughter aren't the reward for a good life — they're the fuel. Start the engine first."
